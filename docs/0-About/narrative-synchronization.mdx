# Narrative Synchronization

The Main Scenario Quest timeline is the chronological blueprint for this
encyclopaedia. Deep-dives are anchored to narrative milestones you reach in your
own playthrough — not ahead of them.

<LorePanel variant="lore" title="Pause at the Milestone">

When you reach a significant MSQ beat, that is the natural moment to read the
entries tied to it. The sidebar order follows this progression so you can stay in
sync with the story.

</LorePanel>
