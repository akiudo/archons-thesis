# Introduction

Welcome to **The Archon's Thesis** — an interconnected lore encyclopedia and
companion to the FFXIV Main Scenario Quest.

<LorePanel variant="lore" title="Start Here">

This project is organized into three reading tracks, each with its own section on the
site:

- **[The Journey](/docs/The-Journey)** — MSQ chapters to read along with your story
- **[The Encyclopaedia](/docs/The-Encyclopaedia)** — thematic deep-dives into the world
- **[Context](/docs/A-Realm-Reborn/Context)** — clarifications and background notes outside the MSQ itself

[Chronicles](/blog) covers site news and project updates — separate from the lore
tracks above.

Read the sections in this category to understand how the project works, then begin
with [The Journey](/docs/The-Journey).

</LorePanel>

## What you will find

- **Narrative synchronization** — deep-dives anchored to MSQ milestones
- **Thematic exploration** — world-building, side narratives, and universe physics
- **Primary sources** — claims tied back to official lore where possible
