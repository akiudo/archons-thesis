# Tour Guide Perspective

Entries are written from a third-person limited point of view that matches your
chronological progression through the MSQ. The goal is to enrich your immediate
experience without exposing late-game plot vulnerabilities.

<LorePanel variant="warning" title="Spoilers Will Be Marked">

We try to avoid spoilers ahead of your natural progression. When they cannot be
avoided — especially in deep-dives — entries will be clearly marked. See the
full policy on the [About](/about) page.

</LorePanel>

## Primary sources

Where possible, entries cite *Encyclopaedia Eorzea*, *Chronicles of Light*, and
in-game text so you can verify claims yourself.
