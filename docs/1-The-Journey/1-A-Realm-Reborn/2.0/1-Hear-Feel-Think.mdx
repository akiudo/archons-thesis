# Hear... Feel... Think...

*This chapter covers the opening moments of **A Realm Reborn** — the dream that plays
during character creation, before you ever set foot in a city-state.*

<ContextRef
  to="/docs/Context/A-Tale-of-Two-Beginnings"
  title="A Tale of Two Beginnings">
  for how this dream fits alongside the ARR opening cinematic and Legacy character
  imports
</ContextRef>


When the adventurer opens their eyes, the darkness is replaced by the sight of a vast
expanse filled with swirling aether. Slowly, they feel themselves falling, drifting
until their boots gently touch an invisible, solid ground.

Standing in this strange realm, they look around in confusion, wondering exactly what
kind of place they have stumbled into. Out of the vast emptiness of the ethereal void,
a gentle female voice echoes—speaking not to their ears, but to their very soul.

_"Hear... Feel..."_

A mysterious light appears in front of the adventurer, its warm orange glow possessing a
strange allure, beckoning them to come closer.

As the adventurer approaches the floating light, a purple miasma erupts between them,
turning the welcoming warmth into a dark and oppressive sight. A hooded figure emerges
from the darkness, blocking the light. The strange man raises his head, revealing a
face obscured by an ornate red mask.

As he stands there—clad in a long coat laced with metal decorations and spiked shoulder
pauldrons—the gentle voice pushes through the intimidating presence, speaking once more
to the adventurer's soul.

_"Hear... Feel... Think..."_

The imposing sight of the dark-hooded man is swallowed by the mysterious light as it
surges toward the adventurer, bringing its warmth ever closer. The oppressive air
dissolves into a gentle embrace as the glow suddenly intensifies. It envelops the
adventurer, burning brighter and brighter, until their world is consumed by a blinding
flash for just a fraction of a second—before dissipating entirely, leaving them with a
lingering sense of profound empowerment.

As this newfound power surges through the adventurer's body, they feel an inexplicable
urge to vanquish the oppressive presence of the mysterious hooded man. With
determination and focus, a weapon of light manifests in their hand as they shift into
an offensive stance, ready to strike.

The dark-hooded man suddenly exudes a purple miasma as dark energy begins to build
around him. He locks eyes with the adventurer just as a glowing crimson glyph
materializes before his mask. The adventurer gathers their own radiant energy, bracing
themselves as the dark figure lunges forward.

The ethereal realm erupts in a blinding flash of light—but as quickly as the clash
begins, the radiance shatters. Darkness takes hold, and the world goes utterly black.
