# The Holy See

*A brief Heavensward placeholder — enough lore names to exercise the Index, not a full chapter.*

After the events of *A Realm Reborn*, the path north leads into **Coerthas**: a highland
once green, now locked in eternal winter. Beyond the snowfields rises **Ishgard**, the
Holy See — a city of stone, faith, and a thousand-year war with dragons.

Further west lie the wilds of **Dravania**, where the great wyrm **Hraesvelgr** still
holds court among the mountains. Companions such as **Alphinaud Leveilleur** walk this
northern road with you. These names will be expanded in proper Journey chapters; for
now they exist so the [Lore Index](/lore-index) can demonstrate expansion-based
spoiler gating.
