# About The Archon's Thesis

**The Archon's Thesis** is an interconnected lore encyclopedia and companion video
essay project dedicated to *Final Fantasy XIV*. It exists to give players deep
world context without the narrative gaps and pacing problems common to long-form
gaming content.

It is **not** a quest-by-quest walkthrough. It is a guided reading of the world —
its history, its powers, and its physics — paced to match your own journey through
the Main Scenario Quest.

## Who this is for

- Players progressing through the MSQ who want richer context as they go.
- Returning players who want to reconnect with the story without a full replay.
- Lore enthusiasts who want primary sources gathered and interpreted in one place.

## How we approach the story

<LorePanel variant="lore" title="Three Guiding Principles">

- **Narrative Synchronization** — The MSQ timeline is our chronological blueprint.
  Deep-dives are anchored to the milestones you reach, never ahead of them.
- **Thematic Deep-Dives** — We explore world-building, side-content narratives, and
  universe physics (from the Aetheryte network to the Coils of Bahamut) rather than
  reciting quests.
- **Tour Guide Perspective** — A third-person limited viewpoint that matches your
  progression, enriched with primary source documentation.

</LorePanel>

## Spoiler policy

<LorePanel variant="warning" title="Spoilers Will Be Marked">

Entries are written from the perspective of a player at the same point in the story
as you, and we deliberately avoid revealing late-game plot developments before you
would naturally encounter them.

That said, spoilers cannot always be avoided — especially in deep-dives into certain
items, quests, and dungeons. **Any post that contains spoilers will be clearly marked
as such**, and where possible spoiler content is gated or deferred to a later entry so
you can choose whether to read on.

</LorePanel>

## Primary sources

Where possible, entries cite in-universe and official documentation, such as the
*Encyclopaedia Eorzea* volumes and the *Chronicles of Light*. Citations appear
inline so you can trace any claim back to its source.

## A small team, real limits

<LorePanel variant="history" title="Run by One Person">

The Archon's Thesis is run primarily by a single person. Every entry — research,
writing, and upkeep — comes from one set of hands doing their best to get it right.

That means mistakes and errors **can and will** appear on this site. Lore is vast,
sources sometimes conflict, and no one catches everything on their own.

</LorePanel>

<LorePanel variant="lore" title="Help Us Improve">

If you spot a factual error, a missed citation, a spoiler that slipped through, or
anything that does not sit right — [Akiudo Suzume](/about-the-author) would genuinely
love to hear from you. Corrections make the encyclopedia better for everyone.

Reach out via the [Contact](/contact) page or open an issue on
[GitHub](https://github.com/controvi/archons-thesis).

</LorePanel>
