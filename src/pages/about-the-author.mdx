# About the Author

<AuthorProfile />

The Archon's Thesis is written by **Akiudo Suzume**, an active *Final Fantasy XIV* player
on Lich [Light]. This project began as a personal desire to dive into the lore
myself — to understand the world deeply as the MSQ unfolded — and grew into something
that might help other players on the same journey as well.

## Why trust this project?

<LorePanel variant="lore" title="A Player, Not a Spectator">

The encyclopedia entries here are researched and written by someone actively playing
through the content they describe. Where claims are made, they are tied to primary
sources — the *Encyclopaedia Eorzea*, the *Chronicles of Light*, and in-game text —
not second-hand summaries or wiki paraphrases.

The Lodestone profile above links to a verifiable in-game character. This is a fan
project by a real player, not an anonymous aggregation.

</LorePanel>

## Background

Akiudo Suzume has spent considerable time in Eorzea across the full breadth of the MSQ and
its surrounding content. What started as personal research — diving into topics that
caught my interest along the way — is now being organised here in the hope it proves
useful to others as well.
