# Privacy Notice

The Archon's Thesis is a personal, non-commercial fan project. This page explains
what data is collected when you visit and why.

<LorePanel variant="lore" title="The Short Version">

This site uses Google Analytics to understand how many people visit and which
entries are read. Your IP address is anonymized, and no personal information is
collected or sold. That is the extent of it.

</LorePanel>

## What we collect

We use **Google Analytics 4** to gather anonymous, aggregated usage statistics,
such as:

- Which pages are viewed and how often
- General visitor counts and trends over time
- Broad technical details like browser type and approximate region

This helps decide which topics to prioritize and whether the site is useful.

## What we do not do

- We do **not** collect your name, email, or any personal identifying information.
- We do **not** sell or share data with third parties beyond Google Analytics.
- We do **not** use tracking to build advertising profiles.

## IP anonymization

IP addresses are anonymized before processing, so your precise address is not
stored or associated with your activity.

## Cookies

Google Analytics sets cookies to distinguish returning visitors and measure usage.
You can block or delete these cookies through your browser settings at any time
without affecting your ability to read the site.

## Managing your data

- **Opt out globally** with Google's
  [browser add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).
- **Block cookies** via your browser's privacy settings.
- Learn more in
  [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Questions

If you have any questions about privacy on this site, reach out via the
[Contact](/contact) page.
