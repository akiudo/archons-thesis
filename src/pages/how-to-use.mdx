# How to Use This Site

The Archon's Thesis is designed to be read **alongside** your playthrough, not
instead of it. Here is how to get the most from it.

## Read in step with the MSQ

Each entry is anchored to a point in the Main Scenario Quest. The recommended rhythm:

1. Play until you reach a narrative milestone.
2. Pause and read the entries anchored to that milestone.
3. Return to the game with richer context.

<LorePanel variant="lore" title="Follow the Sidebar Order">

The **Encyclopaedia** is organized by expansion, era, and topic in chronological
order. Reading top to bottom keeps you in sync with the story and minimizes
spoiler risk.

</LorePanel>

## Web and video, together

The project has two complementary components:

- **The Web Component** (this site) — the written index, wiki database, and script
  repository. Best for reference, citations, and reading at your own pace.
- **The Video Component** — a companion video essay series mapped to these entries,
  best for a cinematic, narrated experience.

<LorePanel variant="warning" title="Video Content Coming Later">

The video essay series does not exist yet — it is planned and will be created later.
For now, the written entries on this site are the complete experience.

</LorePanel>

Once available, use whichever suits the moment; the two will cover the same material
from different angles.

## Interactive elements

- **Database tooltips** — hover linked terms to see official Eorzea Database entries.
- **Source citations** — inline chips point to the primary source behind a claim.
- **Spoiler gates** — some terms are hidden behind a click to protect the unwary.

<LorePanel variant="warning" title="Mind the Spoiler Gates">

If a term or panel is marked as a spoiler, it means the content sits ahead of where
you likely are in the story. Reveal it only if you are comfortable knowing more.

</LorePanel>

## Where to start

New here? Begin with the [About page](/about) for the project's purpose, then open
the **Encyclopaedia** and start from the first entry.
