# Tales from the Source

The **Hingashi Chronicles** are in-character travel logs written alongside
[The Archon's Thesis](/about) — personal stories from three adventurers moving through
the same world the encyclopedia describes, but told from their own perspectives.

Where the Journey pages offer a guided reading of Eorzea's history and systems, these
tales follow **Akiudo Suzume** and her companions through the Main Scenario as lived
experience: field notes, reflections, and the quieter moments between quests.

<LorePanel variant="history" title="Companion Writing, Not Canon">

These stories are creative fiction based on in-game roleplay. They enrich the journey
but do not override official lore or MSQ summaries on this site. When a tale references
events you have not reached yet, treat it as optional colour rather than required reading.

</LorePanel>
