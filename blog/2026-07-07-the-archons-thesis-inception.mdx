Today marks the first public step of a project I have been shaping for some time:
**The Archon's Thesis** — a lore encyclopedia and narrative companion for *Final
Fantasy XIV*, built to be read alongside the Main Scenario Quest.

{/* truncate */}

## Why this exists

This started as something I wanted for myself — a reason to dive deeply into the
lore, the history, and the systems of Eorzea as I made my way through the MSQ.
Along the way, I realised it might help others on the same journey too.

That is what The Archon's Thesis became: a personal deep-dive, shared openly.

## What is live today

The site is now online with:

- An **encyclopaedia** scaffold organized by expansion and era
- Pages on the project's **purpose**, **how to use it**, and **about the author**
- The first lore entry stubs under **A Realm Reborn**

The written web component is the foundation. A companion **video essay series** is
planned for later — the encyclopaedia comes first.

## What comes next

Over the coming weeks I will begin filling in the first real lore entries, starting
with topics like the Aetheryte network and the city-states of Eorzea. Corrections,
feedback, and suggestions are always welcome — this is a living project, run
primarily by one person, and it will improve with help from the community.

If you are making your way through the MSQ and want richer context as you go, I hope
this proves useful.
