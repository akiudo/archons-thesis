# A Tale of Two Beginnings

When you launch *A Realm Reborn*, the opening cinematic tells one story. When you
create a character and step into Eorzea, the game begins with another. This entry
explains that difference — and why both versions exist.

<LorePanel variant="lore" title="Why This Matters">

If you have ever wondered whether you "missed" something between the trailer and
your first quest, you are not alone. Understanding this split is one of the most
useful context pieces for reading the MSQ from the start.

</LorePanel>

## Legacy players vs. new adventurers

*Write here: the design reason for the two openings — why Square Enix kept a
cinematic that does not match every player's backstory.*

| | Legacy (1.0) players | New ARR players |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Prior history | Played through 1.0; character present at the Calamity | None — created after ARR launch |
| Relation to the cinematic | The cinematic **is** their character's past | The cinematic is **backstory for the world**, not personal memory |
| Starting MSQ | Same quests, different implied context | Same quests, fresh arrival framing |
